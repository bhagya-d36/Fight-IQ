# Current UFC Champions

## How current is this list of champions?

This list of champions is current as of July 2026. UFC titles change hands
regularly — re-check current sources and re-run `python ingest.py` after
editing this file whenever a title changes hands.

## Who is the current UFC Heavyweight Champion?

Tom Aspinall (England) has been the undisputed UFC Heavyweight Champion since
June 21, 2025, when he was elevated to undisputed champion after Jon Jones
vacated the title and retired. Aspinall's title reign has been complicated by
injury: an October 2025 title bout against Ciryl Gane at UFC 321 ended in a
no contest after Gane accidentally poked Aspinall in both eyes, and Aspinall
has been out recovering from eye surgery since, without a confirmed return
date.

## Who is the current UFC Interim Heavyweight Champion?

Ciryl Gane (France) won the interim UFC Heavyweight Championship on June 14,
2026, at UFC Freedom 250, defeating Alex Pereira by TKO in the second round.
An interim title is created when the undisputed champion is unavailable for
an extended period (in this case, Aspinall's injury); the interim champion is
expected to eventually unify the belt against the undisputed champion.

## Who is the current UFC Light Heavyweight Champion?

Carlos Ulberg (New Zealand) has been the UFC Light Heavyweight Champion since
April 11, 2026, when he knocked out former champion Jiří Procházka in the
first round at UFC 327 to win the vacant title, extending his win streak to
10 fights in a row.

## Who is the current UFC Middleweight Champion?

Sean Strickland (USA) has been the UFC Middleweight Champion since May 9,
2026, after defeating Khamzat Chimaev by split decision at UFC 328 to hand
Chimaev the first loss of his career. This is Strickland's second reign as
middleweight champion.

## Who is the current UFC Welterweight Champion?

Islam Makhachev (Russia) has been the UFC Welterweight Champion since
November 15, 2025, after defeating Jack Della Maddalena by unanimous decision
at UFC 322. Makhachev moved up from lightweight to welterweight to win this
title and is scheduled to make his first welterweight title defense against
Ian Machado Garry on August 15, 2026, at UFC 330.

## Who is the current UFC Lightweight Champion?

Justin Gaethje (USA) has been the UFC Lightweight Champion since June 14,
2026, after defeating previously unbeaten champion Ilia Topuria at UFC
Freedom 250.

## Who is the current UFC Featherweight Champion?

Alexander Volkanovski (Australia) has been the UFC Featherweight Champion
since April 12, 2025, and has successfully defended the title since.

## Who is the current UFC Bantamweight Champion?

Petr Yan (Russia) has been the UFC Bantamweight Champion since December 6,
2025, after defeating Merab Dvalishvili at UFC 323 to reclaim the title he
had previously lost.

## Who is the current UFC Flyweight Champion?

Joshua Van (Myanmar) has been the UFC Flyweight Champion since December 6,
2025, after a first-round TKO of former champion Alexandre Pantoja at UFC
323.

## Who is the current UFC Women's Bantamweight Champion?

Kayla Harrison (USA) has been the UFC Women's Bantamweight Champion since
June 7, 2025, after a second-round submission win over Julianna Peña at UFC
316.

## Who is the current UFC Women's Flyweight Champion?

Valentina Shevchenko (Kyrgyzstan) has been the UFC Women's Flyweight Champion
since September 14, 2024, after a unanimous decision win over Alexa Grasso at
UFC 306.

## Who is the current UFC Women's Strawweight Champion?

Mackenzie Dern (USA/Brazil) has been the UFC Women's Strawweight Champion
since October 25, 2025, after defeating Virna Jandiroba at UFC 321 in Abu
Dhabi.

## Is the UFC Women's Featherweight title active?

No. The UFC Women's Featherweight Championship has been vacant and the
division largely inactive since former two-division champion Amanda Nunes
stepped away from competition in 2023.
