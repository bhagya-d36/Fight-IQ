# UFC Fight Night: Aspinall vs. Tybura

UFC Fight Night: Aspinall vs. Tybura (also known as UFC Fight Night 224 and UFC on ESPN+ 82) was a mixed martial arts event produced by the Ultimate Fighting Championship that took place on July 22, 2023, at The O2 Arena in London, England.

## Background

The event marked the promotion's 15th visit to London and first since UFC 286 in March 2023.
A heavyweight bout between Tom Aspinall and Marcin Tybura headlined the event.
