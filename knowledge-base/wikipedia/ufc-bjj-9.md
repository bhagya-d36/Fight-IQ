# UFC BJJ 9

UFC BJJ 9: Fowler vs. Johnson was a submission grappling event produced by UFC Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (UFC BJJ) on June 4, 2026, at the Meta APEX in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States. It was broadcast live around the world on YouTube and UFC Fight Pass.

## Background

The main event featured Mason Fowler defending the UFC BJJ light heavyweight championship against Devhonte Johnson.
In the co-main event, former UFC welterweight Gilbert Burns made his UFC BJJ debut against Horlando Monteiro at middleweight.
