# UFC Fight Night: Nurmagomedov vs. Song

UFC Fight Night: Nurmagomedov vs. Song (also known as UFC Fight Night 286) is an upcoming mixed martial arts event produced by the Ultimate Fighting Championship that is scheduled to take place on August 29, 2026, at the SPD Bank Oriental Sports Center in Shanghai, China.

## Background

The event will mark the promotion's third visit to Shanghai and first since UFC Fight Night: Walker vs. Zhang in August 2025. 
A bantamweight bout between former UFC Bantamweight Championship challenger Umar Nurmagomedov and Song Yadong is scheduled to headline this event.
