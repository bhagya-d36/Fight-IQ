# UFC Fight Night: Hernandez vs. Rodrigues

UFC Fight Night: Hernandez vs. Rodrigues (also known as UFC Fight Night 285) is an upcoming mixed martial arts event produced by the Ultimate Fighting Championship that is scheduled to take place on August 22, 2026, at the Golden 1 Center in Sacramento, California.

## Background

The event will mark the promotion's seventh visit to Sacramento and first since UFC Fight Night: de Randamie vs. Ladd in July 2019. There were initial reports that the event would take place at the Meta Apex in Las Vegas, Nevada, but for undisclosed reasons, it was later shifted to Sacramento.
A middleweight bout between former LFA Middleweight Champions Anthony Hernandez and Gregory Rodrigues is scheduled to headline the event.
