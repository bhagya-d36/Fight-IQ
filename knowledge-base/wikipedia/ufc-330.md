# UFC 330

UFC 330: Makhachev vs. Machado Garry is an upcoming mixed martial arts event produced by the Ultimate Fighting Championship that is scheduled to take place on August 15, 2026, at the Xfinity Mobile Arena in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States.

## Background

The event will mark the promotion's fourth visit to Philadelphia, its first event since UFC on ESPN: Barboza vs. Gaethje in March 2019 and first numbered event since UFC 133 in August 2011.
A UFC Welterweight Championship bout between current champion (also former UFC Lightweight Champion) Islam Makhachev and Ian Machado Garry is expected to headline the event. Michael Morales is expected to serve as backup and potential replacement for this fight. 
A UFC Women's Strawweight Championship bout between current champion Mackenzie Dern and Gillian Robertson is expected to take place at the event.
A middleweight bout between Caio Borralho and Joe Pyfer was reportedly being targeted for the event. However, Borralho announced that he was unable to compete due to complications from a rib injury, and the matchup was abandoned.
