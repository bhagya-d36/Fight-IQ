# UFC Fight Night 287

UFC Fight Night 287 is an upcoming mixed martial arts event produced by the Ultimate Fighting Championship that is scheduled to take place on September 5, 2026, at the Accor Arena in Paris, France.

## Background

The event will mark the promotion's fifth consecutive annual visit to Paris and first since UFC Fight Night: Imavov vs. Borralho in September 2025.

## Announced bouts

Women's Strawweight bout: Delphine Benouaich vs. TBA
Featherweight bout: Trevor Peek vs. Kurtis Campbell
Flyweight bout: Fabià Sintes vs. Michael Aljarouj
