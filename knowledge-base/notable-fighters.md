# Notable UFC Fighters

## How current are these fighter records and title statuses?

Records and title statuses in this file are current as of July 2026 —
re-check current sources and re-run `python ingest.py` if this file is
updated.

## Who is Islam Makhachev?

Islam Makhachev is a Russian (Dagestani) mixed martial artist and the current
UFC Welterweight Champion, with a professional record of 28 wins and 1 loss.
A protégé of Khabib Nurmagomedov, he built his career on elite wrestling and
sambo-based grappling. He is a former UFC Lightweight Champion who moved up
to welterweight and won that title in November 2025, extending his win
streak to 16 fights, tying the UFC record held by Anderson Silva.

## Who is Tom Aspinall?

Tom Aspinall is an English mixed martial artist and the current UFC
Heavyweight Champion, with a professional record of 15 wins and 3 losses. He
is known for exceptionally fast, powerful finishes and once held the UFC
record for shortest average fight time. His title reign has been
interrupted by an eye injury sustained in an October 2025 no-contest against
Ciryl Gane.

## Who is Ciryl Gane?

Ciryl Gane is a French mixed martial artist and the current Interim UFC
Heavyweight Champion, nicknamed "Bon Gamin." A former kickboxer known for his
movement and technical striking, he won the interim heavyweight title in
June 2026 by TKO over Alex Pereira, becoming a two-time interim heavyweight
champion.

## Who is Alexander Volkanovski?

Alexander Volkanovski is an Australian mixed martial artist and the current
UFC Featherweight Champion. A former wrestler with well-rounded boxing, he is
widely regarded as one of the greatest featherweights in UFC history and has
held the title since April 2025.

## Who is Merab Dvalishvili?

Merab Dvalishvili is a Georgian mixed martial artist and a former UFC
Bantamweight Champion, with a professional record of 21 wins and 5 losses.
Known for an extremely high pace and elite wrestling-based grappling, he lost
his title to Petr Yan at UFC 323 in December 2025 after multiple successful
title defenses.

## Who is Petr Yan?

Petr Yan is a Russian mixed martial artist and the current UFC Bantamweight
Champion, with a professional record of 20 wins and 5 losses. Known for
sharp, powerful striking, he reclaimed the bantamweight title in December
2025 by defeating Merab Dvalishvili, capping a multi-year comeback after
previously losing the belt.

## Who is Sean Strickland?

Sean Strickland is an American mixed martial artist and the current UFC
Middleweight Champion (his second reign), with a professional record of 31
wins and 7 losses. Known for a high-volume boxing style and outspoken
personality, he won the title in May 2026 by handing Khamzat Chimaev the
first loss of his career.

## Who is Justin Gaethje?

Justin Gaethje is an American mixed martial artist and the current UFC
Lightweight Champion, nicknamed "The Highlight." Known for an aggressive,
fan-friendly brawling style, he won the lightweight title in June 2026 by
defeating previously unbeaten champion Ilia Topuria.

## Who is Carlos Ulberg?

Carlos Ulberg is a New Zealand mixed martial artist and the current UFC
Light Heavyweight Champion, nicknamed "Black Jag," with a professional
record of 14 wins and 1 loss. A former kickboxer with sharp striking, he won
the vacant title in April 2026 by knocking out former champion Jiří
Procházka, extending his win streak to 10 fights.

## Who is Kayla Harrison?

Kayla Harrison is an American mixed martial artist and the current UFC
Women's Bantamweight Champion. A two-time Olympic gold medalist in judo, she
is known for dominant, high-pace grappling and won the bantamweight title in
June 2025 by second-round submission over Julianna Peña.

## Who is Valentina Shevchenko?

Valentina Shevchenko is a Kyrgyzstani mixed martial artist and the current
UFC Women's Flyweight Champion, nicknamed "Bullet," with a UFC/Octagon record
of 15 wins, 3 losses, and 1 draw. Regarded as one of the most dominant
champions in UFC history, she has nine combined successful title defenses
across her reigns, tied for fourth most all-time.

## Who is Mackenzie Dern?

Mackenzie Dern is an American-Brazilian mixed martial artist and the current
UFC Women's Strawweight Champion. A multiple-time Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu world
champion, she has developed increasingly dangerous striking to complement her
grappling, and won the strawweight title in October 2025 by defeating Virna
Jandiroba.

## Who is Joshua Van?

Joshua Van is a mixed martial artist from Myanmar and the current UFC
Flyweight Champion. He shocked the division with a first-round TKO of
long-reigning former champion Alexandre Pantoja in December 2025 to win the
title.
